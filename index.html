LensPay

For merchants that want to process bank cards online or at a physical store without the need for a bank POS system. For shoppers that want to forget their bank cards at home, and never enter them on a website again.

LensPay can make this process feel like a breeze. It really only takes 3 steps:

Create

Merchants create a transaction request via our API or mobile app, and present the generated transaction-specific QR code to the shopper

Scan

Shoppers scan the QR code with the LensPay app, verify the amount requested by the merchant, and confirm

Complete

Notification for a successful transaction is received by both parties, and a receipt is sent to the shopper's email

Features

What can LensPay do for you?

Merchants:

Available in the following countries

Accept cards

Visa, MasterCard, Maestro, American Express... accept all types of bank cards, debit or credit!

App or API

Generate codes for your online store via our API, generate them by hand for printed invoives or mail, or use the app for physical transactions

Follow process

Follow the payment process using the mobile app, from the code generation, to when the shopper scanned and confirmed the payment

Very low fees

We collect only 1% of the total transaction amount, plus the Stripe processing fees that Stripe collects

Shoppers:

Available worldwide!

Liberate your cards

Use any bank card you own in the app, and pay online or offline

Scan codes

No need for typing, scan the transaction from the merchant using your camera, confirm the requested amount, and you are done!

Save card

Save your cards on your phone and never have to enter them again. All information is securely encrypted on the device and never goes to our servers

Pay at any merchant

Pay at any merchant that supports LensPay, let that be a webstore, a physical location, an emailed invoice or even a printed one
